👮 Worst-Case Deployment Optimizer
Ranks every corridor by its worst-case risk at the chosen hour (sweeping all event causes), then distributes the officers on duty across them — and exports a printable shift briefing. Fully standalone, computes the risk ranking internally.
👉 Set the hour & officers on duty, then click Optimize for a ranked deployment.
